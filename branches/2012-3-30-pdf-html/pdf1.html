Membership is open to any resident within the EMCC boundaries and any property owner or operator of a business serving EMCC residents.

Membership shall be for one calendar year beginning January 1 and ending December 31, with dues payable by January 31 for existing members and upon entry to EMCC for new members.

Annual membership dues of EMCC are not refundable.
